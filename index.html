Iolani 84 Reunion at Las Vegas August 22-24

The Journey

I went to Las Vegas to learn something about myself.

I did.

Thirty years ago we shared experiences as kids.

Who I am? What do I value? The 150 history and tradition of the school, the teachers, and city of Honolulu all played a role in defining my answers. But, there was an even bigger influence.

Like trees reaching maturity in the same forest, each of us had an impact on each other as we grew.

I went to Las Vegas to laugh, eat great food, and talk about the old times. I also came to learn from people that made an impact on my life.

Perhaps others did too.

Like we did 30 years ago, we brought our different personalities and experiences into the collective pool of knowledge to help us prepare for the future once again.
