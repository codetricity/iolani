30 Year Reunion Activities Iolani Class of 1984

The Iolani class of 1984 celebrated our 30th reunion in Las Vegas on August 22nd through August 24th. Thanks to the hard work of Chris Yokogawa, the event was a great success.

We had a nice turnout with people flying in from Hawaii and California. Most of the people stayed at the Flamingo, right in the center of the action. One person stayed at the Aria, which worked out nicely as a change in venue for the Saturday night drinks.

An overview of the events:

  • Friday happy hour in the Flamingo hospitality suite
  • Late night partying at a number of Las Vegas night clubs and bars
  • Saturday lunch at the Tilted Kilt
  • High Roller ferris wheel at the LINQ
  • Awesome BBQ dinner at Fogo De Chao
  • Late night partying at the Aria
